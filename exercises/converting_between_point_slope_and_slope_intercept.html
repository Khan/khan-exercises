Convert the following equation from point slope to slope intercept form.

In other words, if the equation is rewritten to look like y = mx + b, what are the values of m and b?

y - y1 = m(x - x1)

m = m

b = b

Multiply out the m(x - x1) term on the right.

y - y1 = expr([ "*", m, "x" ]) - var1

Isolate the y term on the left.

y = expr([ "*", m, "x" ]) - var1 + y1

Factor terms on the right.

y = expr([ "*", m, "x" ]) + b

Inspecting the equation in slope intercept form, we see the following:

m = m

b = b

Both equations could represent the same line!
