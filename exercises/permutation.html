Playing a game with avail plural(item, avail) (toSentence(avail_colors, 'and')), you can put pick of them into slots the order of your choice. How many permutations are available to you?

nperm

You can choose any of the avail colors to fill the only slot available.

You can choose any of the avail colors to fill the first slot. Since you cannot use the color again, you only have avail - 1 choices available for the second slot, and so forth. You need to multiply together avail, avail - 1, etc, until you run out of slots.

You only multiply by numbers you have slots for. If you had slots, you would have avail! possibilities. Then, you divide by the (avail-pick)! that you don't have room for.

Note that 0! is 1, and that in this example you do not run out of slots because you have avail slots to fill with pick plural(item, avail).
