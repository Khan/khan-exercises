person(1) is a race driver on a straight track (like in Fast and Furious). he(1) starts accelerating with ACC\frac{m}{s^2} and keeps that rate until the finish line. he(1) crosses the line after TIME seconds. What was his(1) top speed?

TOP_SPEED

The top speed is achieved at the end of the race, TIME seconds after the start.

Since the track is straight the direction of the acceleration is allway the same (forward) and therefore the direction of the velocity is the same.

The acceleration is the velocity divided by the time \vec{a} = \frac{\vec{v}}{t} . Therefore the velocity is the time multiplied by the acceleration \vec{v} = \vec{a}\times t

\vec{v} = \vec{a}\times t = ACC\frac{m}{s^2}\times TIMEs = TOP_SPEED\frac{m}{s}
