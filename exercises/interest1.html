person(1) borrowed $PRINCIPAL from the Bank today, and must repay the loan in one payment, PERIOD yearplural(PERIOD) from now. The load carries a compound interest rate of INTEREST_RATE% per year. How much money does person(1) need to repay?

FUTURE_VALUE

The amount person(1) needs to pay is calculated by taking the principal amount person(1) borrowed, and compounding it by the interest rate i each year for the duration of the loan, n.

In other words, Future Value = Present Value * (1 + i)^n

Substituting our values, Future Value = PRINCIPAL * (1 + INTEREST_RATE/100 )^ PERIOD

Future Value = FUTURE_VALUE
