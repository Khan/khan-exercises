randRange(5, 9) randRange(16, 25) ((LOWER + UPPER) * (UPPER - LOWER + 1))/2

Evaluate: \sum\limits_{k=LOWER}^{UPPER} k

ANSWER

We know that \sum\limits_{k=1}^{n} k = \dfrac{n(n+1)}{2}

But notice that k starts at LOWER and \sum\limits_{k=LOWER}^{UPPER} k = LOWER + LOWER+1 + LOWER+2 + \cdots + UPPER-1 + UPPER

If k started at 1, then \sum\limits_{k=1}^{UPPER} k = 1 + 2 + 3 + \cdots + UPPER-1 + UPPER
(which is similar to what we need to find...)

By grouping sums together, the series could also be written as \sum\limits_{k=1}^{UPPER} k = (1 + 2 + 3 + \cdots + LOWER-2 + LOWER-1) + (LOWER + LOWER+1 + LOWER+2 + \cdots + UPPER-1 + UPPER)

Rewriting the right side as 2 sums gives \sum\limits_{k=1}^{UPPER} k = \sum\limits_{k=1}^{LOWER-1} k + \sum\limits_{k=LOWER}^{UPPER} k

That last sum is what we're looking for! Time to simplify.

\dfrac{UPPER \times UPPER+1}{2} = \dfrac{LOWER-1 \times LOWER}{2} + \sum\limits_{k=LOWER}^{UPPER} k

(UPPER*(UPPER+1))/2 = (LOWER*(LOWER-1))/2 + \sum\limits_{k=LOWER}^{UPPER} k

ANSWER = \sum\limits_{k=LOWER}^{UPPER} k

The sum is therefore ANSWER.
