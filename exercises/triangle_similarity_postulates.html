Side side side (SSS) similarity

The lengths of segments AB and XY are at a ratio of SIDEMULT_A to SIDEMULT_B.

Side BC corresponds to side YZ and side AC corresponds to side XZ.

{AB\over XY} = {SIDEMULT_A\over SIDEMULT_B} = {BC\over YZ} = {AC\over XZ}

Due to the Side Side Side (SSS) similarity postulate, the triangles are similar to each other.
