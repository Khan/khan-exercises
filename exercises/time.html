PIECH.drawLabels();

The bigger hand is the minute hand.

The bigger hand is pointing to (MINUTE / 5) === 0 ? 12: MINUTE / 5 , and that is equal to MINUTE minutes.

The smaller hand is the hour hand

It is pointing at HOUR so it is HOUR o'clock.

It is between HOUR and HOUR + 1 === 13 ? 1 : HOUR + 1 so it is HOUR o'clock.

It is close to HOUR + 1 === 13 ? 1 : HOUR + 1 but because the minute hand has not yet entered the new hour, it is still HOUR o'clock.
