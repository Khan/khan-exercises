The cosine of an angle is equal to the adjacent side over the hypotenuse.

\mathrm{cos}(ANGLE^{\circ}) = \vec{v}_x/VELOCITY

\vec{v}_x = \mathrm{cos}(ANGLE^{\circ}) \times VELOCITY

\vec{v}_x = roundTo(1, cos(ANGLE * PI / 180)*VELOCITY) \space m/s

\vec{v}_x \approx roundTo(1, cos(ANGLE * PI / 180)*VELOCITY) \space m/s
