The sum of two vectors is the vector obtained by adding the respective components, i.e., adding the first two components of each vector, the second two components of each vector, and so on.

In particular, the sum of two vectors with four components is given by the following formula:

\left[\begin{array}{cccc}\color{orange}{a_1} \\ \color{blue}{a_2} \\ \color{red}{a_3} \\ \color{green}{a_4}\end{array}\right]+ \left[\begin{array}{cccc}\color{orange}{b_1} \\ \color{blue}{b_2} \\ \color{red}{b_3} \\ \color{green}{b_4}\end{array}\right]= \left[\begin{array}{cccc} \color{orange}{a_1+b_1}\\ \color{blue}{a_2+b_2}\\ \color{red}{a_3+b_3}\\ \color{green}{a_4+b_4}\end{array}\right]

Using the formula, we get:

\left[\begin{array}{cccc}\color{orange}{A1} \\ \color{blue}{A2} \\ \color{red}{A3} \\ \color{green}{A4}\end{array}\right]+ \left[\begin{array}{cccc}\color{orange}{B1} \\ \color{blue}{B2} \\ \color{red}{B3} \\ \color{green}{B4}\end{array}\right]= \left[\begin{array}{cccc} \color{orange}{A1 + B1}\\ \color{blue}{A2 + B2}\\ \color{red}{A3 + B3}\\ \color{green}{A4 + B4} \end{array}\right]

Calculating each term, we get:

\left[\begin{array}{cccc} \color{orange}{A1 + B1}\\ \color{blue}{A2 + B2}\\ \color{red}{A3 + B3}\\ \color{green}{A4 + B4} \end{array}\right] = \left[\begin{array}{cccc} \color{orange}{C1}\\ \color{blue}{C2}\\ \color{red}{C3}\\ \color{green}{C4} \end{array}\right]
