What is the result of the following binary expression?

A_BIN \oplus B_BIN
SOLUTION

\oplus is the symbol of exclusive disjunction, or the "XOR" operation, also known as nonequivalence.

To determine the XOR of two binary numbers, proceed bit by bit and write the XOR of corresponding bits of the two numbers to the result.

The XOR of bits is 1 if they aren't equal and 0 otherwise.

A_BIN \oplus B_BIN = SOLUTION_BIN
