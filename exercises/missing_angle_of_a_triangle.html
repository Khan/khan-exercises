Given angle X in picture below is X° and Y is Y°. What is the degree of Z?

(Extra Info: the degrees of three angles inside the triangle add up to 180°.)

init({ range: [[-4, 6], [-4, 6]] }); var angles = [X, Y, 180 - X - Y]; var triangle = new Triangle( [0, 0], angles, 5, {'angles' : ['X', 'Y', 'Z']}); triangle.draw(); triangle.drawLabels();

180 - X - Y

From extra info above we know since X, Y, Z are three different angles of a triangle, then (degree of X) + (degree of Y) + (degree of Z) = 180°. Here we know degree of X = X° and degree of Y = Y°, so what is degree of Z?

degree of Z = 180° - (degree of X) - (degree of Y) = 180° - X° - Y° = 180 - X - Y°
