Given two angles inside the triangle are X and Y degrees, respectively. What is the degree of third angle?

init({ range: [[-4, 6], [-4, 6]] }); var angles = [X, Y, 180 - X - Y]; var triangle = new Triangle( [0, 0], angles, 5, {'angles' : [X, Y, "?"]}); triangle.draw(); triangle.drawLabels();

180 - X - Y

Remember that the degrees of three angles inside the triangle add up to 180.

In other word, if X, Y, Z are three different angles of a triangle, then X + Y + Z = 180. And here we know X = X and Y = Y, so what is Z?

Z = 180 - X - Y = 180 - X - Y = 180 - X - Y
