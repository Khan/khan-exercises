Solve the system of equations represented by the augmented matrix \begin{bmatrix} \begin{array} {ccc|c} A & B & C & D \\ E & F & G & H \\ I & J & K & L \end{array} \end{bmatrix}

You can try to simplify the matrix with row reduction below:

matrix = new Matrix( [ [A,B,C,D], [E,F,G,H], [I,J,K,L] ], $('#matrix') ); matrix.show();

times row onto row

x_1 = X1

x_2 = X2

x_3 = X3

The system of equations represented is

(A)\color{red}x_1+(B)\color{blue}x_2+(C)\color{purple}x_3=D
(E)\color{red}x_1+(F)\color{blue}x_2+(G)\color{purple}x_3=H
(I)\color{red}x_1+(J)\color{blue}x_2+(K)\color{purple}x_3=L

The row reduced echelon form of the matrix is when:
  • All nonzero rows have leading entries of 1.
  • The leading 1 of each nonzero row occur to the right of the leading 1 of the previous row
  • all other entries aside from the leading 1s are zero
In row reduced echelon form, the matrix essentially represents the answer to the system of equations:

(1)\color{red}x_1+(0)\color{blue}x_2+(0)\color{purple}x_3= ?
(0)\color{red}x_1+(1)\color{blue}x_2+(0)\color{purple}x_3= ?
(0)\color{red}x_1+(0)\color{blue}x_2+(1)\color{purple}x_3= ?

The first row is the answer to x_1, the second is x_2, and the third is x_3.
