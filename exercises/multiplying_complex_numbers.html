Complex numbers are multiplied like any polynomials.

(A_REAL + A_IMAGi) \cdot (B_REAL + B_IMAGi) = (A_REAL \cdot B_REAL) + (A_REAL \cdot B_IMAGi) + (A_IMAGi \cdot B_REAL) + (A_IMAGi \cdot B_IMAGi)

The imaginary units on can be grouped together. The real and imaginary parts are normally multiplied. (A_REAL * B_REAL) + (A_REAL * B_IMAG + A_IMAG * B_REAL)i + (A_IMAG * B_IMAGi^2)

The result is simplified. (ANSWER_REAL + ANSWER_IMAGi)
