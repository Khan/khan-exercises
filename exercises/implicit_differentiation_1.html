\frac{dy}{dx} = ?

\frac{reduce(-2*coeffX,2*coeffY)[0]x} {reduce(-2*coeffX,2*coeffY)[1]y}

  • \frac{reduce(r-2*coeffX,2*coeffY)[0]x} {reduce(r-2*coeffX,2*coeffY)[1]y}
  • \frac{reduce(r+2*coeffX,2*coeffY)[0]x} {reduce(r+2*coeffX,2*coeffY)[1]y}

Let's take the derivative of both sides.

\frac{d}{dx}coeffXx^2+coeffYy^2=\frac{d}{dx}r

\frac{d}{dx}coeffXx^2+\frac{d}{dx}coeffYy^2=\frac{d}{dx}r

Let g=coeffYy^2
According to the chain rule, \frac{dg}{dx} = \frac{dg}{dy}*\frac{dy}{dx}

So, \frac{d}{dx}coeffYy^2=\frac{dg}{dx} = \frac{dg}{dy}*\frac{dy}{dx}

\frac{d}{dx}coeffXx^2+\frac{dg}{dy}*\frac{dy}{dx}=\frac{d}{dx}r

\frac{dg}{dy}=\frac{d}{dy}coeffYy^2=2*coeffYy

Now solve for \frac{dy}{dx}:

\frac{d}{dx}coeffXx^2+2*coeffYy*\frac{dy}{dx}=\frac{d}{dx}r

2*coeffXx+2*coeffYy*\frac{dy}{dx}=0

2*coeffYy*\frac{dy}{dx}=-2*coeffXx

\frac{dy}{dx}=\frac{ -2*coeffXx}{2*coeffYy} =\frac{reduce(-2*coeffX,2*coeffY)[0]x} {reduce(-2*coeffX,2*coeffY)[1]y}
