The magnitude of the electric force is proportional to the magnitude of the multiplication of both charges.

Charges with the same sign repel; charges with different sign attract.

According to Coulomb's Law the electric force in the original situation is: F_{12} = k \times \frac{q1 \times q2}{d^2}.

According to Coulomb's Law the electric force in the new situation is: F_{12}' = k \times \frac{CHARGE_FACTORq1 \times q2}{d^2}.

The relation between the original and new force is the following: F_{12}' = CHARGE_FACTOR \times F_{12}.
