If the two angles are formed by two intersecting lines and they are across from each other, then they are vertical angles.

If the two angles share a ray, do not overlap each other, and do not form a straight line, then they are adjacent angles.

If the two angles share a common ray and the other two rays are opposite rays (form a straight angle), then they form a linear pair.
