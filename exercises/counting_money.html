We are going to count up all the dollars and then count up all the cents.

The twenties are: TWENTIES * $20 or $fixeddecimals(TWENTIES*20,2)

The tens are: TENS * $10 or $fixeddecimals(TENS*10,2).

The fives are: FIVES * $5 or $fixeddecimals(FIVES*5,2).

The ones are: ONES * $1 or $fixeddecimals(ONES,2)

All of these together add up to SOLN - ALLCOINS/100 dollars.

The quarters are: QUARTERS * 25 cents or 25*QUARTERS cents.

The dimes are: DIMES * 10 cents or 10*DIMES cents.

The nickels are: NICKELS * 5 cents or 5*NICKELS cents.

For the pennies, each penny is worth 1 cent.

All of these together add up to ALLCOINS cents.

Put together the dollars and the cents for an answer of $ SOLN.
