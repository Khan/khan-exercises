Complex numbers can be visualized as points on the Gauss plane. The coordinates on the real and imaginary axes correspond to the real and imaginary parts of the complex number.

REAL + IMAGi has real part REAL and imaginary part IMAG.

style({ stroke: "red", strokeWidth: 2.0 }); line( [ REAL, LOWER_BOUND - 1 ], [ REAL, UPPER_BOUND + 1 ] ); movablePoint.visibleShape().toFront();

The red line represents all complex numbers with real part REAL (including REAL + IMAGi).

style({ stroke: "blue", strokeWidth: 2.0 }); line( [ LOWER_BOUND - 1, IMAG ], [ UPPER_BOUND + 1, IMAG ] ); movablePoint.visibleShape.toFront();

The blue line represents all complex numbers with imaginary part IMAG, also including REAL + IMAGi.
