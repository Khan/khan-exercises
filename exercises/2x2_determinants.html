DET

Remember, to find the determinant of a 2X2 Matrix, you multiply opposite terms and subtract the off diagonal terms from the diagonal terms.

\left| \begin{array}{rcl} a&b\\ c&d\\ \end{array} \right| = ad - bc

\left| \begin{array}{rcl} A[0][0]&A[0][1]\\ A[1][0]&A[1][1] \end{array} \right| = (A[0][0])(A[1][1]) - (A[0][1])(A[1][0])

= A[0][0]*A[1][1] - A[0][1]*A[1][0]

= A[0][0]*A[1][1]-A[0][1]*A[1][0]
