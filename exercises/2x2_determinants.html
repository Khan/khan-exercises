Remember, to find the determinant of a 2x2 matrix, subtract the product of the off diagonal terms from the product of the main diagonal terms.

\left| \begin{array}{rcl} \color{BLUE}{a}&\color{GREEN}{b}\\ \color{ORANGE}c&\color{PINK}d\\ \end{array} \right| = \color{BLUE}{a}\color{PINK}{d} - \color{GREEN}{b}\color{ORANGE}{c}

\left| \begin{array}{rcl} \color{BLUE}{A[0][0]}&\color{GREEN}{A[0][1]}\\ \color{ORANGE}{A[1][0]}&\color{PINK}{A[1][1]} \end{array} \right| = \color{BLUE}{(A[0][0])}\color{PINK}{(A[1][1])} - \color{GREEN}{(A[0][1])}\color{ORANGE}{(A[1][0])}

= A[0][0]*A[1][1] - A[0][1]*A[1][0]
