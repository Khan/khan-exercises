A projectile is launched at an angle of angle degrees with an initial velocity of velocity m/s.

What is the initial velocity in the horizontal direction? You may round your answers to the nearest tenth.

roundTo(1,(cos(angle)*velocity))

If we break down the vector into its vertical and horizontal componenets, we can see that it forms a right triangle.

Now we can use our trig functions to find the horizontal velocity.

cosangle = vx/velocity

vx = cosangle \times velocity m/s
