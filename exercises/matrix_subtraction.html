To subtract one matrix from another, perform the subtraction component by component, that is, subtract the upper left components of each matrix, the upper rights components, and so on.

\left[\begin{array}{cccc}\color{orange}{a_{11}}& \color{blue}{a_{12}}\\ \color{red}{a_{21}} & \color{green}{a_{22}}\end{array}\right]- \left[\begin{array}{cccc}\color{orange}{b_{11}}& \color{blue}{b_{12}}\\ \color{red}{b_{21}} & \color{green}{b_{22}}\end{array}\right]= \left[\begin{array}{cccc} \color{orange}{a_{11}-b_{11}}& \color{blue}{a_{12}-b_{12}}\\ \color{red}{a_{21}-b_{21}} & \color{green}{a_{22}-b_{22}}\end{array}\right]
