Remember that the name of an angle is made of the three letters that represent the angle's three points.

The letters must match exactly. (If the diagram shows uppercase letters, the answer should have uppercase letters.)

The letter that represents the vertex of the angle should always be in the middle of the name.

The vertex of the angle is the point where the two lines meet. In this problem, the vertex is represented by labels[1].
