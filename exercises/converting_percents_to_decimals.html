Express the percent as a decimal.

PERCENT\%

DECIMAL

PERCENT percent = PERCENT per cent = PERCENT per hundred

PERCENT\% = \dfrac{PERCENT}{100}

\hphantom{PERCENT\%} = DECIMAL

Another way to convert a percent to a decimal is to divide by 100 (or move the decimal two places to the left) and remove the percent sign.
